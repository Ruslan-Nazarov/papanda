# 📊 papanda Dashboard & Widgets Guide

The papanda Dashboard is an interactive workspace designed for organizing your thoughts, learning foreign languages, managing your time, and building daily habits. Each widget is engineered to foster mindfulness and help you manage your daily activities without unnecessary friction.

Most widgets on the dashboard share a **standard set of capabilities**:
- **Fold / Unfold**: Allows you to collapse details and keep only the essentials visible on your screen.
- **Custom Border Colors**: Helps you visually group widgets or highlight them by priority.
- **Drag & Resize**: You can rearrange and resize widgets freely across your workspace.
- **Visibility Control**: Any widget can be enabled or disabled anytime via the **Settings** page.

---

## ⏳ 1. Chronology

The **Chronology** widget is designed for recording events, memories, and important moments of your life.

* **Daily Practice**: Aim to write down your memories of the previous day every morning.
* **Default Calendar Date**: Note that when you open the widget, the calendar defaults to **yesterday's date**, making it ideal for morning reflection.
* **Additional Features**: 
  * View your entire event timeline with a powerful search tool.
  * Use the rich text editor for detailed journaling.
  * Fold the widget or customize its border color.
* *This widget can be disabled on the Settings page.*

## 🌍 2. Words (Language Learning)

**Words** is an independent foreign language learning module. papanda focuses on three core pillars: notes (dialectics), languages, and time management. Language learning is integrated directly into your dashboard for seamless daily practice and exposure.

The learning process is built on **two key principles**:
1. **Learning to understand sentences**: By mastering syntax and real-life phrases, you acquire the language naturally.
2. **No rote memorization**: Instead of mechanical memorization, you train word "encounter frequency" and build a rich personal context.

This philosophy is implemented through three main "games" and tools:

### 🔹 Word Triplets
* By default, words are presented in triplets. Even if you aren't actively studying a specific language, you build context and visual familiarity without rote memorization.
* **Personal Context & Meanings**: Russian translations are shown by default, but most importantly, you can and should add your own personal associations and meanings that resonate with you. You can also fix typos or errors right here.
* **List Management**: Refresh the triplet manually by clicking the arrow, or set an automatic rotation interval in Settings.
* **Learned Words**: You can mark an entire triplet as familiar, and it will no longer appear. Your total count of learned triplets is tracked in **Words Statistics** under the *Fully Learned* counter.
* You can switch your three studied foreign languages in Settings.

### 🔹 Recognition Test
* **Test Goal**: Rather than testing mechanical memory or exact translations, this test checks whether you recognize the visual form of a word and remember encountering it.
* You can customize test parameters: adjust the question count, regulate the repetition ratio of learned words, or hide translations across all languages for a bigger challenge.
* If all displayed words are familiar, you can mark them all as learned with a single click.

### 🔹 Phrase Trainer
* The centerpiece of the module, based on learning language through sentence structure.
* **Interactive Word Order**: Tap the central area to make words appear sequentially in their correct grammatical order (subject, predicate, etc.).
* You can modify existing sentences or add your own custom phrases.
* Attach **stickers** to any language to record your grammar notes, language patterns, and helpful tricks.

### 🔹 Statistics
* Statistics serve not only for tracking progress but also as an active learning tool (e.g., the **Frequent Words** section).
* Each statistical metric comes with helpful methodological notes and tips.

## 📌 3. Stickers

**Stickers** play a vital unifying role on the dashboard—they link different widgets together and promote deep mindfulness regarding your tasks and habits.

* **Two Sticker Types**: Action/task lists or concise text notes.
* **Sticker Board**: The widget features a dedicated board where all stickers created through this widget are pinned.
* **Global Management**: From here, you can access and manage **all stickers across the entire application** (including those created inside calendars, tasks, or habits). You can edit, delete, and search across your entire collection.
* Change sticker colors and drag them smoothly across the board by clicking and holding.

---

## 📅 4. Today (Calendar & Schedule)

The **Today** widget serves as your operational calendar for keeping track of your upcoming schedule.

* **Main Widget View**: Displays calendar events for **Today**, followed directly by events for **Tomorrow**.
* **Full Calendar**: To view other dates, tap the calendar icon in the widget header to open the Full Calendar (offering identical functionality in an expanded layout).
* **Task Management**: Within the widget, you can drag tasks, edit, delete, mark them as completed, or attach stickers.
* **Sticker Indicators**: When an event has attached stickers, its icon turns yellow and displays the total sticker count.
* **Full Calendar Extras**: Gain access to global search across all dates and view **event trees** (events grouped by color tag).

---

## 🌲 5. Activity Overview (Live Observation / Activity Tree)

This widget serves a dual purpose: planning your time and evaluating your actual daily time distribution.

* **Tree Structure**: Build a tree to design your ideal daily schedule OR record how you actually spend your time throughout the day (you can effectively combine both approaches).
* **Multiple Trees**: Create multiple independent activity trees and switch between them effortlessly.
* **Detailed View Window**: Open the full tree view in a separate window or jump directly to the activity scheduled for the current time.
* **Activity Parameters**: When adding an activity, specify:
  * Whether it occurs at a specific time or if time is flexible.
  * Whether it links to a specific task from the **Tasks** widget (aligning goals with time).
  * Execution duration and specific days of the week.
* **Automatic Time Sync**: Every time the page reloads, the tree automatically scrolls and focuses on the activity relevant to the current time of day.

---

## 📝 6. Notes

The **Notes** widget is your personal knowledge base for capturing ideas on any topic.

* **Categories**: Create custom categories for different topics to keep your thoughts structured.
* **Dashboard Pinning**: Pin your most important or work-in-progress notes directly to the dashboard widget for instant access.
* **Rich Editor**: Edit notes in quick mode or use the full rich text editor with formatting tools.
* **Global Search & Management**: Access your complete collection of notes with powerful search, category sorting, editing, and deletion tools.
* Attach **stickers** to any note (e.g., for article outlines, editing checklists, or brainstorming).

---

## 🔥 7. Habits

The **Habits** widget tracks continuous day streaks for your habits, beneficial routines, or recurring events (complementing the top menu counters).

* **Mindfulness via Stickers**: Attach stickers to any habit to record observations, track triggers or slip-ups, and brainstorm improvements.
* **Quick Control**: Mark a habit as completed for today with a single click, or jump to the full habit list with built-in search.
* Supports standard features like custom border colors and display adjustments.

---

## 🎯 8. Tasks

The **Tasks** widget helps you maintain unwavering focus on your primary goals and build clear action hierarchies.

* **Main Task**: Set your **Main Task** along with a backup goal (to take its place if your primary plan changes).
* **Day Counter**: An automatic day streak counter runs for your Main Task, showing how long you've maintained focus.
* **Decomposition**: Break down your Main Task into smaller sub-tasks, each tracked with its own independent day counter.
* **Life Overview Integration**: Mark tasks as completed; they are tightly integrated with the **Life Overview** module.
* Attach stickers to tasks to detail action plans or record quick insights.

---

## ⬆️ 9. Top Menu

The top navigation bar is your command center for quick data entry and monitoring:
* **Quick Input**: Instantly add data to almost any widget (except Words and Stickers) from anywhere in the application.
* **Before & After Counters**: Track elapsed time since past milestones or countdown to upcoming events.
* **Winks**: Gentle, unobtrusive reminders that don't demand immediate action. Glancing at them periodically reinforces important thoughts, actions, or long-term goals in your memory.
* **Language Switcher**: Instantly switch the interface language (RU / EN / KZ).
* **Notes / Dialectics Access**: Direct gateway to the dialectical notes module.

---

## ⬇️ 10. Bottom Menu

The bottom bar (footer) provides access to system settings, help, and community resources:
* **Settings**: Navigate to application and widget configuration pages.
* **Tutorials & Help**: Access user guides, documentation, and video tutorials.
* **About & Feedback**: Learn about the project philosophy, contact the developer directly (via Telegram or Email), and follow the application update history (**Changelog**).
